## Chat & Content Rules

1. **Be Respectful**  
   Treat all players with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discriminatory language, or personal attacks are not allowed.

2. **No Spamming**  
   Do not flood chat with repeated messages, characters, or irrelevant content.

3. **Appropriate Language and Content**  
   Chat messages, player names, item names, and other user-created content must be suitable for all ages and not offensive.

4. **No Advertising**  
   Advertising other servers, websites, or services without staff approval is prohibited.

5. **Follow Staff Instructions**  
   Staff instructions must be followed at all times.

6. **Use English in Global Chat**  
   English must be used in global chat so all players can understand conversations.

## Gameplay Rules

1. **Fair Play**  
   Play in a fair and reasonable manner. Actions taken solely to ruin or degrade the experience of others are not allowed.

2. **No Cheating**  
   Using modified clients, external tools, or methods that provide an unfair advantage is prohibited.

3. **No Exploiting**  
   Bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics may not be abused.

4. **No Griefing**  
   Deliberately destroying or sabotaging other playersâ€™ work for malicious reasons is not allowed.

5. **No Harassment Through Gameplay**  
   Repeatedly targeting a player in ways intended to annoy, intimidate, or disrupt them is prohibited.

6. **Server Stability**  
   Intentionally causing lag, crashes, or instability is not allowed.

## Economy Rules

1. **Good-Faith Trading**  
   Economic interactions must be conducted honestly and clearly.

2. **No Scamming**  
   Intentionally deceiving players for personal gain is prohibited.

3. **No Duplication**  
   Duplication of items or currency through any means is not allowed.

4. **No Alt Abuse**  
   Alternate accounts may not be used to gain unfair advantages.

## Enforcement

1. **Staff Discretion**  
   Staff may act on behavior that violates the spirit of these rules.

2. **No Rule Evasion**  
   Attempts to bypass rules or punishments will result in harsher penalties.
